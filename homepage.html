What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cybercrime where attackers impersonate legitimate organizations to steal sensitive information like passwords, credit card numbers, and personal data. These attacks typically come through email, text messages, or fraudulent websites.

Cybercriminals use sophisticated techniques to make their fake communications look authentic, often creating urgency to trick victims into acting quickly without thinking.
